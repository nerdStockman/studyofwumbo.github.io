About the Foundation

This is just the beginning.

H.A.W.K Flight Foundation began as an idea during graduate school and grew into action through leadership, community, and a commitment to giving back.

✓ Designed for students pursuing aviation careers.
✓ Focused on both flight and maintenance training.
✓ Powered by donors, mentors, industry partners, and community members.
